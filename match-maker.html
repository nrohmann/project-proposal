Match Maker

by Nicholas Rohmann.

Hello and welcome to Matchmaker. Are you having trouble finding your soulmate? Tired of spending countless hours and money on dates, only to find our he or she is not the one? Who hasn't endured this struggle? Lucky for you, you have Matchmaker. With Matchmaker, you'll skip the wait, agony, and misery of unbecoming relationships and breakups. So what are you waiting for? Jump right in and get ready to find the love of your life!

Instructions: 1. Open Matchmaker web app in browser. 2. You will be presented with five statements. You are constrained to respond only with the following options: 'Strongly Disagree', 'Diasgree', 'Neutral', 'Agree', and 'Strongly Agree'. 3. Respond to all statements. Once finished, your 'Total Weighted Compatibility' score will be revealed.
